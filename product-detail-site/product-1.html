L'Enigma del Sacro Cuore

€250.00 In Stock (1 available)

This work combines the symbolism of the Sacred Heart with the visual and cultural richness of the pomegranate, a fruit deeply rooted in Sicilian tradition.

The pomegranate, opened and pierced by a sacred heart transfigured into a blade, exudes both vitality and fragility.

The intense red background amplifies this tension between pain and sacredness, recalling the balance between sweetness and bitterness that inhabits my artistic universe.

Through this composition, I explore the dialogue between spirituality, Mediterranean tradition, and contemporary expressiveness, inviting the viewer to decipher its intimate and symbolic enigma.
