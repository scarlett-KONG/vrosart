Commission

The process begins by filling out the form below.

Once your requests and information have been processed, typically within 3 business days, I'll email you an invoice and a 50% deposit to confirm the commission, payable by card or PayPal.

After 50% non-refundable deposit I will create a rough sketch of the concept for your approval before paintings begins and will keep you updated. Please note the initial 50% deposit is non-refundable, as it covers the time, planning, and materials invested up to that point.

The remaining 50% is due upon completion.

Minor revisions can be made after the sketch stage. Once painting begins, small adjustments are possible.

Oil painting process takes 4-6 weeks (depending on size and details) plus time to dry 10-15 days.

Delivery time depends on country you're located, it's about 1-2 weeks.

Free worldwide shipping.
